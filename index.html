Self Design Critique

Sometimes there's nobody around to critique your design. Or maybe you're too insecure to have your feelings crushed today. This is the tool for those ocassions.

Just answer the questions while looking at your design, trying to be objective, mercyless, even harsh. Hopefully it will make the weak spots really clear.

To contribute questions, send an email to axel@paskola.com, mention @axel on Twitter, or make a pull request at GitHub.
